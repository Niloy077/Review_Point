The Vanishing Half

Author: Brit Bennett


THE VANISHING HALF, the next novel from the stunning National Book Award 5 Under 35 recipient Brit Bennett, begins when Desiree Vignes returns to her hometown of Mallard, Louisiana, with a daughter who looks nothing like her and no husband. No one has seen Desiree, or her identical twin sister Stella, since they disappeared from town and reappeared in New Orleans attempting to create new lives.
